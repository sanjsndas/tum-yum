Privacy Policy

Welcome to Tumyum! We are a fun-first food platform dedicated to blending street food, snacky bites, fusion recipes, and playful food culture into one vibrant, scroll-worthy experience. Your privacy is incredibly important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Tumyum ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users ("you" or "users") of the Tumyum.com website ("Site") and all products and services offered by Tumyum. By using our Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. This policy applies to the Site and all products and services offered by Tumyum.

We understand that when you share your information with us, you are placing your trust in us. We take this responsibility seriously and are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy is designed to be transparent about our data practices, helping you make informed decisions about your information when you interact with Tumyum. We encourage you to read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information from and about users of our Site, including information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular consumer or household ("Personal Information"). The types of information we collect depend on how you interact with our Site and services.

1.1. Information You Provide to Us Directly

  • Account Registration Data: When you create an account on Tumyum, we may collect your username, email address, password, and any other information you provide during the registration process. This information is essential for managing your account and providing personalized services.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information for your profile, such as your name, profile picture, dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free), cooking interests, and location. This helps us tailor content and recommendations to your tastes.
  • Content Submissions: If you submit recipes, blog posts, comments, photos, videos, or other content to our Site, we collect the information you provide within that content. This includes text, images, and any associated metadata.
  • Communications: When you communicate with us via email, customer support, or other channels, we collect the content of your communications and any contact information you provide (e.g., your name, email address, phone number).
  • Interactive Features Data: If you participate in our interactive extras like the "Snack Personality Quiz," "Tumyum Generator," "SnackStyle Avatars," or "Mini Game: Tumyum Tap," we may collect data related to your responses, preferences, and game activity. This data helps us improve these features and understand user engagement.
  • Survey and Feedback Data: From time to time, we may ask you to participate in surveys or provide feedback. We collect the information you provide in response to these requests, which helps us understand user satisfaction and improve our services.
  • Transaction Data: If we introduce paid services or products in the future (e.g., premium recipes, merchandise), we would collect information necessary to complete your transactions, such as billing address, shipping address, and payment details (though actual payment processing would be handled by secure third-party payment processors).

1.2. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit and interact with our Site, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity.

  • Log Data: Our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Site. This Log Data may include your Internet Protocol ("IP") address, browser type and settings, the date and time of your request, how you used the Site, and cookie data.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our Site, including the device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your activity on the Site, such as the pages you visit, the recipes you view, the features you use, the time spent on certain pages, and your clickstream data. This helps us understand popular content and areas for improvement.
  • Location Information: We may derive your approximate location from your IP address. If you access our Site via a mobile device and grant us permission, we may collect precise geolocation data.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, analyze site usage, and deliver personalized content. For more details, please refer to our Cookies Policy.

1.3. Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media Data: If you choose to link your Tumyum account with a third-party social media service (e.g., Facebook, Instagram), we may receive information from that service, such as your public profile information (name, profile picture, user ID) and any other information you permit the social media service to share.
  • Analytics Providers: We may receive aggregated or anonymized data from third-party analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) about user behavior on our Site. This data does not directly identify you.
  • Advertising Partners: If we engage advertising partners, they may provide us with information about your interactions with their ads on our Site or other platforms, which helps us measure ad effectiveness.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Tumyum uses "cookies" and other similar technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and deliver personalized content and advertising. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. It allows the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, font size, and other display preferences) over a period of time, so you don't have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your device until they expire or you delete them). We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of our Site. They enable you to navigate the Site and use its features, such as accessing secure areas of the Site. Without these cookies, services like logging into your account cannot be provided.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our Site, such as which pages you visit most often and if you encounter any error messages. They help us understand and improve the performance of our Site. For example, we use Google Analytics to track website traffic and user behavior.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our Site to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced, more personalized features. They can also be used to remember changes you have made to text size, fonts, and other parts of web pages that you can customize.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements more relevant to you and your interests. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. They are usually placed by advertising networks with our permission.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. However, if you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of our Site. For more detailed information about cookies and how to manage them, please refer to our Cookies Policy.

We are committed to providing you with control over your Personal Information. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your data.

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to request access to the Personal Information we hold about you and to request corrections to any inaccuracies. You can often access and update your account information directly through your Tumyum profile settings.
  • Deletion: You may have the right to request the deletion of your Personal Information, subject to certain legal exceptions. Please note that deleting your account may remove your public contributions (e.g., recipes, comments) from the Site, but we may retain certain information for legal or legitimate business purposes (e.g., backup, fraud prevention).
  • Opt-Out of Marketing Communications: You can opt out of receiving promotional emails from us by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in those emails. Even if you opt out, we may still send you non-promotional communications, such as those about your account or our ongoing business relations.
  • Cookie Preferences: As mentioned in Section 4, you can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • Do Not Track Signals: Some web browsers offer a "Do Not Track" ("DNT") signal. Currently, our Site does not respond to DNT signals.
  • Data Portability: In some jurisdictions, you may have the right to request a copy of your Personal Information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Objection to Processing: You may have the right to object to the processing of your Personal Information in certain circumstances, such as for direct marketing purposes.
  • Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process your Personal Information, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.
